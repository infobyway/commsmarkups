Bachelor of Science in Information Science (BSIS): InfoSci @ College Park

The Bachelor of Science in Information Science undergraduate degree is offered as a four-year program at the University of Maryland College Park campus for new students and transfer students. A Data Science specialization is also available at our College Park location. Out of over 90 Majors offered at the University of Maryland, the InfoSci program is one of five Computing Majors focusing on data management and analytics, user experience, and information sciences.

InfoSci Curriculum at College Park

The InfoSci degree requires a total of 120 credits, including 40 credits in General Education and 45 credits in the Information Science major. In addition to the ten core courses, 15 credits (five courses) of upper level major electives are required to complete the Information Science degree.

A Data Science specialization is available.
